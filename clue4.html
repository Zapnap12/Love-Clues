Our First Dance

New Year’s Eve 2021 — the stars didn’t sparkle more than you did.
We danced, we laughed, and you kissed me like I was already yours.

And as the music played…
Our feet moved, but our hearts danced more.

We didn’t need steps.
We needed smiles.

A song of old love for a love that just began.
You were tipsy, I was drunk —
but only on you.

I remember the way your eyes said
“Don’t you dare let me go.”

So I didn’t.
I won’t.
I never will.

Save the last dance, always, for me.

26 hours. 5 countries. 22 beers. 4 hours sleep. And there she was.

She wore her best clothes. Her hair was perfect. I probably smelled like airplane seats and she looked like an angel.

Her first kiss. My first nervous one.
We fumbled with the masks like they were puzzles.
It was short, awkward... and it melted something in me.
She panicked. I smiled big. And the world changed for ever.

26 hours. 3 countries. 2 sandwiches. 0 sleep. And there she was.

She wore her best clothes. Her hair was perfect. I probably smelled like airplane seats and ambition.

Her first kiss. My hundredth nervous one.
We fumbled with the masks like they were puzzles.
It was short, awkward... and it melted something in me.
She panicked. I grinned. And the world changed.

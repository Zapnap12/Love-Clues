The music played, soft and slow…
You took my hand without a word.
The room was full, but I saw no one else.
Just you, and the spark we stirred.
Your smile, the clink of glasses near…
But my heart? It danced with yours alone.
We twirled through seconds, kissed through time,
And I knew — this dance was home.
So darling, though the music fades…
Save the last dance, always, for me.

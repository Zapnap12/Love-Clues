Clue Two ♥️

Two memories. Two hearts. Two riddles to solve before the next chapter reveals itself.

What word did you always say when you needed translation?
Before you spoke in perfect lines,
You needed help to bridge the signs.
A word you made, so pure, so sweet—
Our laughs began where hearts would meet.
What was the country I found you in during the pandemic?
In stillness, fate began to dance,
Between two lands, a fleeting chance.
I found you there, in quiet grace—
A heart I knew. A sacred place.
